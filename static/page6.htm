How to use




For Bottle Refill

- Remove the cap by twisting.

- Pour into an empty Tide bottle.

- Use as directed on the bottle.

Measurement Guide

- For medium loads, pour 1.5 oz (measuring cup) or fill to bar 1 (dosing cap).

- For large loads, pour 2 oz (measuring cup) or fill to bar 3 (dosing cap).

- For full loads, pour 4 oz (measuring cup) or fill to bar 5 (dosing cap).

How To Use Free & Gentle

- Add your selected clothes into the washer. Remember not to overload the drum.

- Pour required amount of Tide Liquid Detergent into the dispenser or directly into the drum using a dosing cap.

- Select any cycle you want, turn on the washing machine and let Tide do its job.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How is HE Turbo Clean different from Safe-for-HE compatible detergents?

A: Using detergents that are only HE compatible produces excessive suds, which make the machine run longer due to additional rinse cycles needed to rinse out the suds. Tide designed its new HE Turbo Clean detergents with Smart Suds technology, which eliminates suds so the cleaning ingredients in the detergent can act faster and rinse out quicker. Additionally, the wrong detergent can add up to 10 more gallons of water to your HE wash cycle.

Q: Can I save energy by washing in cold water with Tide HE Turbo Clean laundry detergent?

A: Yes. In fact, if all Las Vegas households switched to washing in cold for one month, you could power the Strip for up to six days*! Just imagine the savings you could see in your household by doing the same.

*Based on total electricity use in the United States and all US households switching all loads from warm to cold temperature in standard top-loading machines with electric water heaters.

Q: Is this detergent low suds?

A: Yes. It’s been formulated with Smart Suds technology, which allows you to use even the quick cycle without sacrificing the clean!

Q: Does Tide HE Turbo Clean clean in cold water?

A: Yes. In fact, Tide’s trusted cleaning ingredients are so powerful that it cleans even better* in cold water than the competition does in similar wash conditions.

*Stain removal of 1 dose of Tide vs. 6 doses of the leading competitor liquid brand base variant

Q: Is Tide HE Turbo Clean safe to use with both my whites and colors?

A: Yes. Tide HE Turbo Clean helps keep your whites white and your colors colorful, wash after wash.

Q: Should I use this detergent in a standard washing machine?

A: We recommend only using Tide HE Turbo Clean detergents in high efficiency machines.
