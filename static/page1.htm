All About Sensitive Skin

What is Sensitive Skin?

Most dermatologists characterize sensitive skin as skin that experiences redness, irritation, stinging, dryness, itching and blotchiness. Sensitive skin can also be prone to breakouts and rashes, and almost every case includes inflammation.

All About Sensitive Skin

Possible Causes.

Sensitive skin can be the result of any number of things, including genetics, environmental factors like exposure to sun or harsh weather, skin irritants found in certain facial products, like cleansers, make up and others, and it can also be caused by stress.

All About Sensitive Skin

What Can Help.

Naturally gentle ingredients are essential to your skins health. Thats why Burts Bees incorporates cotton extract, rice extract and aloe, into products to help soften, moisturize and soothe a sensitive skin face.
