2.0 out of 5 starsCheap, poor quality, and poor aesthetics.
ByJordanon May 18, 2015
Size: 6 Pack|Verified Purchase
If you're looking for cheap, simple notebooks, these are certainly them. Unfortunately, they aren't even very good at that. The pages are of very poor quality and ink/pencil tends to smudge everywhere. Also, they are not perforated (which did not affect my rating), but for some reason when you try to fold the notebooks back a lot the sides of the pages closes to the spiral get entirely bent up, resulting in this huge mess of a folder that doesn't close properly and has unsightly bulges. It drives me crazy!

As others have mentioned, the covers are also extremely flimsy. Again, understandable for the price, but that really isn't much of an excuse - notebooks are often used for school, which means they get moved A LOT. You have to have something sturdier in order to hold up, and these covers just bend and fold at the slightest touch.

Overall, I will definitely not be purchasing these again.
